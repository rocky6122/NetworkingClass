2 Player top-down shooter

Both players view the game from their side of the field.

V = Player
t = Opponent

* = Laser
# = Wall

The controls are:
-Arrows Keys to move
-spacebar to shoot (Shoots in the direction you're moving)
-esc to leave

To play one person must host and once the second player joins the game will start.
Aim and shoot at the other player. If you hit them a small animation will play then they will respawn at their 'base'.
No win condition currently exist, just endless fun!

4 Events to look for:
SpawnEvent
MoveEvent
ShootEvent
DieEvent
